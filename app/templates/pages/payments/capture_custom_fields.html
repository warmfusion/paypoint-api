Capture custom fields

As a business do you have fields that you want or need to capture from your customer alongside the payment information? T&Cs acceptance, for example? Promotional Codes – do you need to understand how many people cashed in on last month’s offer? Customer Account Details – is this customer registered in our CRM? Product Information – what are they buying today? Would you like to understand payment trends in relation to these items?

You can configure all API and Hosted requests to capture Custom fields that are relevant to your business.

Within Hosted you can even go as far as to configure:

  • the type of field; Text entry field, multiple radio button selection, multiple dropdown selection or simply additional text on page (Hosted)
  • the content of the field: default values, options available, masked values (Hosted)
  • text for the field label (Hosted)
  • the name of the field that we’ll send back to you in responses, call-backs and notifications (API and Hosted)
  • the position of the field on the hosted page; form top (before the payment capture fields) or form bottom (after the payment capture fields).(Hosted)

We’ll save the values your customer enters and send them back to you in responses, call-backs and notifications.

Your account can also be configured so these fields are available in Reporting. Let us know if you want this feature enabled.
