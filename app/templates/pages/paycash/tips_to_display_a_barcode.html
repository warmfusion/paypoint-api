Tips for displaying the barcode

Here are some handy tips to deliver the PayCash barcode, invoice and associated information in an effective manner in order to ensure successful payment conversion.

  • Tell your cutomers how close they are to one of PayPoint’s 25,000 outlets. You can link direct to a search facility for finding the nearest outlet at www.paypoint.co.uk/paypointlocator. Alternatively you could integrate directly with PayPoint’s centralised web service for location nearest PayPoint outlets. See Outlet Locator.
  • Confirm how much the customer must pay to complete the order. Provide a transaction reference in case of subsequent queries.
  • Let them know exactly how to redeem the invoice – include guidance that if they can’t print the barcode they can make a note of the 19-digit number and provide this in store.
  • Ensure the PayPoint logo is on the printable invoice – it’ll help customers spot the logo at participating stores.
  • Add a clear, visible JavaScript button so the customer can print the invoice.
