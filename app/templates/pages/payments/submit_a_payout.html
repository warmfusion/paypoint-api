Submit a payout

If you’re a Gaming Merchant and need to send your Customer’s winnings back to their payment method then the Payout request will allow you to do just that as a Visa CFT (Cardholder Funds Transfer) or a MasterCard PoW (Payment of Winnings).

You don’t need to reference an original payment or authorisation in order to request a Payout.
