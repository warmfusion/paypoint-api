CardLock

CardLock allows you to capture your customer’s card details whilst remaining PCI complaint. We will provide you with a JavaScript library to embed into your site which, when integrated, securely transfers the card number (and optionally the card security code) to our tokenisation service. We’ll exchange the card number for a CardLock token which you can then use to complete your customer’s payment, authorisation or payout request.

CardLock needs to be enabled on your account and we need to issue you credentials that will allow you to access our CardLock tokenisation service. Please ask our Client Management team if you would like this product enabled.

Once you’re set up you’ll be able to submit your customer’s first payment, authorisation or payout using a CardLock token and all subsequent requests with the merchant token.
