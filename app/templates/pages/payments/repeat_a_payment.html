Repeat a payment (or authorisation)

Do you offer goods or services on a recurring basis to your customers? Do you accept scheduled payments for your customer? You can simplify the submission of the repeated payments by using the Repeat request. You’ll need to flag the very first payment or authorisation request as a repeatable transaction and the repeats themselves will need to reference the original – using your reference or ours.

Your account can be configured to not require a CV2 for Payment or Authorisation requests. Let us know if you want that feature to be enabled.
