Issue a cash barcode

The first step to processing a PayCash transaction is to submit a PayCash Issue request via our API. As part of the request you will need to send details of your customer. This can include name, address, email address etc but at a minimum just needs you to provide a unique reference.

The PayCash issue request should also include the URL you want us to send notification of the completed cash payment.
