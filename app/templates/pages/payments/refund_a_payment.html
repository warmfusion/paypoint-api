Refund a payment

Do you need to return funds back to your customer’s original payment method? Use the Refund request to return the full original sales amount (don’t send an amount on the Refund request) or a smaller amount (specify the amount on the Refund request). You’ll need to specify the reference for the original payment so we can ensure the refunded amount(s) doesn’t exceed the amount of the original payment. Refunds that that do exceed the original amount will be declined.
